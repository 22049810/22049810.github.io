Web Developer skills and Qualifications

A successful Web Developer candidate will have various prerequisite skills/qualifications needed for duties such as a

Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or related field (Master’s preferred). Some skills include, but are not limited to the following:

1. Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to establish and maintain close working relationships with business stakeholders and other technologists

2. Functional knowledge and coding experience

3. Basic knowledge of search engine optimisation (SEO)

4. The ability to multi-task within the budgets and time constraints set by the company

5. Strong communication and problem-solving skills

6. An understanding of development approaches and methods

7. Knowledge of mobile applications, computer operating systems and web browsers

8. Strong analytical skills
